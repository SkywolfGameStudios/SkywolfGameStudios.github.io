Data Safety Policy

DATA SAFETY

Effective Date: 8/22/2011

Skywolf Game Studios is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users and players. We respect your rights to control your personal data and we comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding data protection.

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal data when you use our games, websites, and other services. It also describes your choices and rights regarding your data.

What data do we collect?

We collect data that you provide to us when you create an account, play our games, contact us, or participate in our surveys, contests, or events. This data may include your name, email address, username, password, profile picture, game preferences, achievements, progress, and feedback.

We also collect data that is generated automatically when you use our services, such as your IP address, device information, browser type, operating system, game performance, session duration, and gameplay data.

We may also collect data from third-party sources, such as social media platforms, advertising networks, analytics providers, and payment processors. This data may include your demographic information, interests, online behavior, purchase history, and preferences.

How do we use your data?

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services, such as delivering games, updates, patches, and customer support.
  • To personalize your experience, such as tailoring the content, features, and advertisements to your preferences and interests.
  • To communicate with you, such as sending you notifications, newsletters, promotions, and invitations.
  • To monitor and analyze our services, such as measuring the performance, usage, and effectiveness of our games, websites, and marketing campaigns.
  • To protect our services, such as detecting, preventing, and resolving fraud, abuse, security, and technical issues.
  • To comply with our legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests from authorities, courts, or regulators.
  • To fulfill any other purpose that is compatible with the above purposes, or that you consent to.

How do we store and secure your data?

We store your data on our servers and cloud platforms, which are located in various countries around the world. We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or loss. We also limit the access to your data to our employees, contractors, and partners who need it to perform their duties.

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services to you, or as required by law. When we no longer need your data, we delete it or anonymize it.

How do we share your data?

We may share your data with the following parties for the purposes described above:

  • Our affiliates and subsidiaries, who are subject to this policy or similar privacy practices.
  • Our service providers and partners, who help us with the delivery, maintenance, analysis, and improvement of our services. These include hosting providers, cloud providers, analytics providers, advertising networks, payment processors, and social media platforms.
  • Other users and players, who may see your public profile, game activity, and interactions within our services.
  • Public authorities, courts, or regulators, when we are required or permitted by law to disclose your data.
  • Other parties, with your consent or at your direction.

We do not sell your data to anyone, and we do not share your data with any third parties for their own purposes, unless you agree to it.

What are your choices and rights?

You have the following choices and rights regarding your data:

  • You can access, update, or delete your account and profile data at any time through our services or by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe link in our emails or by changing your account settings.
  • You can manage your cookie preferences and settings through your browser or device.
  • You can request a copy of your data, or ask us to correct, restrict, or erase your data, by contacting us.
  • You can withdraw your consent to our use of your data at any time, by contacting us or by changing your account settings.
  • You can lodge a complaint with a data protection authority if you are unhappy with how we handle your data.

How do we update this policy?

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our websites or by sending you an email. Your continued use of our services after the update constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

How to contact us?

If you have any questions, comments, or requests regarding this policy or your data, please contact us at: SkywolfGameStudios@gmail.com
